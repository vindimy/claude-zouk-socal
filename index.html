What is Brazilian Zouk?

Brazilian Zouk is a partner dance born in Brazil, celebrated worldwide for its flowing movements, deep musical connection, and intimate lead-follow dialogue. Whether you're lacing up your dance shoes for the first time or you're a seasoned Zouk dancer ‚Äî SoCal's Zouk community is here for you.
